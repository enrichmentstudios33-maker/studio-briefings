## ENRICHMENT FANBASE PULSE
*(Ages 6–11 | No preschool trends)*

- **ADVENTURE-BUSINESS** — Kid creator culture is exploding: ~80% of under-11s watch YouTube, and summer camps centered on LEGO animation, game design, and entrepreneurship are sold out for 2026. COPPA 2.0 cleared the Senate (March 2026), raising the protected age to 17 and banning targeted teen ads — parents are newly energized around "intentional" media and teaching kids to *make* content, not just consume it. Mystery/puzzle collectibles (Rubik's × Tetris collabs, blind-box unboxing) remain top spend for this lane. ([Toy Association](https://www.toyassociation.org/ta/toys/research-and-data/reports/trend-spotting.aspx) | [Kidscreen](https://kidscreen.com/2025/10/27/how-kids-creators-are-making-youtube-work-again/))

- **STYLE-SPARKLE** — "Fairytale Core" is the dominant 2026 girls' fashion macro-trend: dreamy pastels, delicate florals, heart motifs, voluminous tutu silhouettes, and everyday shimmer fabrics (metallic accents, light sequin tees). The palette is drifting toward richer, warmer lavenders, blush-golds, and neon pinks — a direct fit for **Birka's lavender-gold lane**. Kawaii unicorn/fairy/dragon prints remain strong, but **gem-sparkle textures** (the **Mira** lane) are trending up as an elevated "sparkle with depth" alternative to flat glitter. ([Lola and the Boys](https://lolaandtheboys.com/blogs/news/kids-fashion-trends-2026) | [Stelle](https://stelleworld.com/blogs/activewear-guides/fashion-trends-kids))

- **PARENT-BUYER** — "Type C parenting" is dominating parenting TikTok: intentional, connection-over-control parents who consciously choose media that nurtures rather than lectures. Black mom content communities are centering warmth-forward parenting and actively seeking joyful, culturally affirming kids' media — not cautionary tales. On Reddit's r/Parenting (850K+ comments analyzed Jan–Jul 2025), screen-time quality trumps quantity; parents reward shows and products that spark offline play or creative output. Intentional parents are also the buyers of enrichment kits, book clubs, and learning toys — a direct pipeline to your audience. ([The Bump](https://www.thebump.com/a/rising-parenting-trends) | [ABC News](https://abcnews.com/GMA/Family/type-parenting-taking-tiktok-parenting-today/story?id=121594045))

- **COMP-FANDOM** — *Bluey*-adjacent "cozy adventure" fandom remains the gold standard benchmark: emotionally safe characters, cooperative play, and family dynamics that respect kids' intelligence. *Craig of the Creek* and *Hilda* fans consistently overlap in "underrated gems" discourse on Archive of Our Own and fan wikis (active April 2026). *Gravity Falls* maintains a strong nostalgia-meets-mystery throughline for ages 9-11, and the *DuckTales* × *Gravity Falls* crossover fandom is a reliable signal for adventure-mystery-treasure-hunt IP appetites. KPop's growth (225M+ global fans, 3× spend vs. other fandoms) is beginning to bleed into kids' animation aesthetics — character design with idol-style visual polish is trending. ([Kidscreen Summit/License Global](https://www.licenseglobal.com/entertainment/how-kids-are-moving-the-entertainment-needle-inside-kidscreen-2026))

- **COMP-FANDOM (continued)** — *Proud Family: Louder and Prouder* energy — culturally specific, multi-generational, joyful — maps closely to what Black mom communities are requesting on TikTok. Studios that lean into specificity (neighborhood, food, family dialect) are winning this audience vs. generic "inclusive" aesthetics.

**Key Takeaway:** The **Mira gem-sparkle lane** is ideally positioned — gem-sparkle is rising over flat glitter, intentional parents want "sparkle with substance," and the Fairytale Core trend validates the full aesthetic system right now.

## AI TOOLS FOR BANKSHEAD ANIMATIONS

### 🚨 URGENT — SORA SHUTTING DOWN IN 48 HOURS
- **OpenAI Sora app shuts down April 26, 2026** — OpenAI announced March 24 that Sora is being discontinued; the app closes in **two days**. The Sora API remains live until September 24, 2026. If any Bankshead workflows use Sora, migrate immediately. ([Wikipedia/Sora](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sora_(text-to-video_model)) | [Webiano](https://webiano.digital/chatgpt-images-2-0-became-the-product-sora-never-managed-to-be/))

### OpenAI
- **ChatGPT Images 2.0 / gpt-image-1.5** launched in April — stronger dense text rendering, multilingual captions, finer detail, and more reliable instruction following. Best-in-class for storyboard illustration, style-guide assets, and character sheet generation. ([ReleaseBot](https://releasebot.io/updates/openai/chatgpt))
- **GPT-5.4 mini** rolling out to Free/Go users with "Thinking" mode for scripting/ideation. New Pro tier ($100/month) bridges Plus ($20) and Pro ($200). ([ChatGPT Plus Pricing](https://chatgptpluspricing.com/))

### Google
- **Veo 4 released April 2026** — the most significant video AI launch this month. Adds: storyboarding (define scene sequences with prompts + camera angles), 30-second video length, 4K resolution, zero-shot avatar creation, and dramatically improved character consistency across cuts. Available via Google Flow and Gemini Ultra. **Directly relevant for animatics and scene blocking.** ([Google Developers Blog](https://developers.googleblog.com/introducing-veo-3-1-and-new-creative-capabilities-in-the-gemini-api/) | [Veo 4 Overview](https://www.veo3ai.io/blog/veo-4-release-everything-you-need-to-know-2026))
- **Veo 3.1 Lite** — cost-effective model at <50% the price of Veo 3.1 Fast, same speed. Good for high-volume rough-cut generation. ([Google AI Dev](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/video))
- **Google Vids update** — now integrates Veo 3.1 + Lyria 3 (music) + AI avatars directly in the browser-based video editor. Free tier available.

### Adobe Firefly
- **Firefly AI Assistant launched April 15, 2026** — conversational agent that orchestrates multi-step workflows across Photoshop, Premiere, Lightroom, Illustrator, and Express from a single prompt. Huge for pipeline efficiency. ([Adobe News](https://news.adobe.com/news/2026/04/adobe-new-creative-agent))
- **Kling 3.0 and Kling 3.0 Omni** added as partner video models inside Firefly — now has 30+ partner models including Google, OpenAI, Runway, and Kling. Firefly becomes a one-stop animation studio interface. ([Creati.ai](https://creati.ai/ai-news/2026-04-16/adobe-firefly-ai-assistant-photoshop-premiere-creative-cloud/))
- **Enhance Speech** (noise reduction/dialogue cleanup) now baked into the browser video editor — useful for cleaning scratch audio tracks.

### Runway ML
- **Gen-4.5** is the current release. Director Mode simulates camera movements and actor positioning before generation. Motion Brush paints motion paths directly onto objects/characters. Best-in-class for branded character animation with intentional timing. ([Runway News](https://runwayml.com/news))

### Midjourney
- Still image generation remains leading quality for character design, background art, and style exploration. Video (V1) capabilities expanding but lacks frame-level precision for commercial animation — best used upstream for concept and storyboard assets.

### Kling AI
- **Kling 3.0 / 3.0 Omni now integrated inside Adobe Firefly** (see above). Standalone Kling wins on cost-per-generation for long-run/high-volume tests. Accessible pricing makes it good for iteration before committing to Runway.

### Pika
- **Pika 2.5** — fastest batch generation (7.5 min vs. Runway's 20 min for comparable sequences). Pikaswaps, Pikadditions, physics simulation, and lighting enhancements. Good for rapid concept animation and client-preview cuts.

### ElevenLabs
- **April 7, 2026 update** — expanded non-Latin script support for instant voice cloning (Hebrew, Thai, Armenian, Georgian, Malayalam, Telugu, Gurmukhi). Voice quality API now returns `recording_quality` and `labelling_status` fields for pipeline QA. Professional cloning (30+ min audio) recommended for character voices. ([ElevenLabs Changelog](https://elevenlabs.io/docs/changelog/2026/4/7))

### HeyGen
- **New "quality" Video Translation model** delivers more natural lip-sync and context-aware translations — accessed via `/v2/video_translate` API with the `mode` parameter. Now supports ElevenLabs V3 Voice Model in avatar video API. Strong for dubbing character videos into multiple languages.

### Hedra
- Supports ElevenLabs + Cartesia model integration for voice cloning. Best for talking-head character animation from still images — useful for social content with Enrichment character faces. ([Hedra Blog](https://www.hedra.com/blog/ai-lip-sync-video-guide))

### CapCut / ByteDance
- **Dreamina Seedance 2.0** (launched March 26) now rolling out in CapCut — text/image/reference-video to video generation. Strong on realistic textures, movement, and lighting. Currently rolling out in Brazil, Indonesia, Malaysia, Mexico, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/26/bytedances-new-ai-video-generation-model-dreamina-seedance-2-0-comes-to-capcut/))
