## AI TOOLS FOR BANKSHEAD ANIMATIONS

*Prioritizing last 48 hours. Urgent items flagged.*

- **⚠️ URGENT — OpenAI Sora app shuts down April 26, 2026 (3 days away).** If any Bankshead workflows use Sora, export all content now at `sora.chatgpt.com/exports/me`. The Sora API follows on September 24, 2026. Disney reportedly had a $150M deal tied to Sora that is now unwinding. Shutdown reason: compute costs (~$1M/day), user base declined to <500K. ([The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/openai-sets-two-stage-sora-shutdown-with-app-closing-april-2026-and-api-following-in-september/), [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/20001152-what-to-know-about-the-sora-discontinuation))

- **OpenAI GPT-Image-2 / ChatGPT Images 2.0 launched April 21, 2026** — 2K resolution, native reasoning, multi-image consistency, and near-perfect text rendering inside images (+316 point improvement over prior models). Currently holds the widest lead in AI image generation benchmarks ever recorded. Directly relevant for storyboard panels, character sheet generation, and title card/logo compositing. ([Medium/Arfaj](https://moarfaj.medium.com/openai-just-dropped-a-bomb-on-the-ai-image-world-and-google-didnt-see-it-coming-20612db7bf30), [BuildFastWithAI](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/chatgpt-images-2-0-gpt-image-2-2026))

- **Adobe Firefly AI Assistant launched April 15, 2026** — A new "creative agent" that orchestrates multi-step workflows across Photoshop, Premiere, Illustrator, Lightroom, and Express via a single conversational interface. Unlimited AI generation now included in plans. Runway video also bundled in Firefly subscription ($10/month). Directly useful for 2D asset generation, color correction, and video compositing pipelines. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/15/adobes-new-firefly-ai-assistant-can-use-creative-cloud-apps-to-complete-tasks/), [Adobe Blog](https://blog.adobe.com/en/publish/2026/04/15/introducing-firefly-ai-assistant-new-way-create-with-our-creative-agent))

- **Google Veo 3** — First generative AI system combining integrated voiceover, facial expressions, sound effects, and music in one model (announced Google I/O 2025, now actively deployed). For animation, this is the most complete end-to-end video generation pipeline available — animate a scene, add character voice and ambient audio simultaneously. Gemini-integrated. High priority for Bankshead to evaluate for animatic/rough-cut workflows.

- **Runway ML Gen-4.5** — Now available via API. Supports both text-to-video and image-to-video (2–10 seconds). Key new feature: supply a first-frame image + text prompt for directed output. Useful for scene continuation and storyboard-to-animatic conversion. ([Runway Changelog](https://runwayml.com/changelog), [Releasebot](https://releasebot.io/updates/runwayai))

- **Kling AI 3.0** (launched Feb 5, 2026) — Complete architectural overhaul; first AI video model with native multimodal unified training (text + image + audio + video in one model). Produces up to 15-second clips — longest of any text-to-video generator. Competitive alternative to Sora for longer scene generation. ([UlazAI](https://ulazai.com/ai-video-models-guide-2025/))

- **Midjourney** — Added "describe" command (prompt generation from visuals). However, it has fallen out of the top 15 on AI image generation benchmarks — GPT-Image-2 and others have overtaken it. Still viable for stylized concept art but no longer the default recommendation for production-grade image work.

- **ElevenLabs** — Voice cloning remains core to full-stack character audio pipelines. Integrated into most professional AI animation workflows for character voice consistency. No major April 2026 update noted but stable platform.

- **HeyGen** — Real-time interactive AI avatars now capable of responding live during product demos and video presentations. Useful for character demos and pitch decks for investor/licensing presentations. Pricing stable.

- **Hedra** — Lip-sync avatar video generation starting at $8–15/month. Cost-effective for character dialogue animatics. ([Gaga.art](https://gaga.art/blog/lip-sync-ai/))

- **Microsoft MAI models launched** — MAI-Transcribe-1, MAI-Voice-1, and MAI-Image-2 now live via Microsoft Foundry and MAI Playground. Direct OpenAI/Google competition in voice and image generation. Pricing TBD but enterprise-focused.

- **Pika** — Generates 3–10 second video clips. Still active in the market but no major April 2026 update noted. Positioned as accessible/fast-iteration tool.

**Key Takeaway:** The Sora shutdown (April 26) is the most urgent operational item — migrate any Sora-dependent workflows to **Runway Gen-4.5** or **Kling 3.0** immediately. For image generation, **GPT-Image-2** just became the new production standard and should be tested in the Bankshead storyboarding pipeline this week. **Adobe Firefly AI Assistant** (April 15 launch) is the sleeper pick — its native Creative Cloud integration makes it the lowest-friction upgrade for teams already in the Adobe ecosystem.
