## 3. AI TOOLS FOR BANKSHEAD ANIMATIONS

- **URGENT — OpenAI Sora shutting down**: Bloomberg (Apr 1) reports Sora is being shuttered; **Kling AI, RunwayML, and Vidu** are absorbing its user base — migrate any Sora-dependent workflows immediately. Kling and RunwayML are the top replacements.
- **Google Veo 3**: First gen-AI system combining voiceover + facial expressions + sound effects + music in one output — major leap for low-budget animation production; evaluate for Bankshead character dialogue scenes.
- **Magic Hour (Q1 2026 launch)**: All-in-one platform combining image editing, animation, and video generation — worth a trial run for storyboard-to-animatic pipeline.

**Key Takeaway:** Sora sunset is URGENT — test Kling AI and Veo 3 this week as primary replacements.

## 4. LICENSING & MERCH RADAR

- **Licensed toy market hits $28.82B in 2026** (up 5.9% YoY); licensed toy sales up 18% H1 2025 — Lilo & Stitch, SpongeBob, and Bluey leading. Plush + collectible hybrid is the winning format.
- **Plush trend**: Limited-edition, serialized, and seasonal plush are converting from toys to collectibles — strong case for Enrichment Studios doing small-batch character drops vs. mass retail to build scarcity/hype.
- **Craft kits / stationery**: American Girl's reimagined 14.5" collector dolls show heritage + modern redesign storytelling works; stationery/craft kit adjacency is underserved for Black girl characters in the 6–11 space.

**Key Takeaway:** Position Enrichment plush as **collectible-first, limited drops** to ride the serialized scarcity wave — avoid mass-retail dilution early.
